Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026  ·  FunApps

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information Voyager collects, how it's used, and what choices you have.

Data Storage

All trip data you enter in Voyager — including trips, activities, restaurants, accommodations, transport, and budget information — is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server.

Location

Voyager requests access to your device's location solely to display your current position on the trip map. Your location is:

  • Used only while the app is open and in use
  • Never stored, recorded, or transmitted
  • Never linked to your identity
  • Never used for advertising or tracking

You can deny or revoke location access at any time in your iPhone Settings. The app remains fully functional without it.

Notifications

If you enable push notifications, reminders are scheduled locally on your device using iOS. No notification data is sent to or processed by any external server.

Calendar Access

If you enable calendar sync, Voyager writes trip events directly to your device's native calendar. We do not read your existing calendar events, and no calendar data is transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

Voyager uses Nominatim (OpenStreetMap) to search for locations when you add a destination. Search queries (place names) are sent to Nominatim's public API. No personal information is included in these requests. Please refer to the Nominatim usage policy for more details.

Children's Privacy

Voyager does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
