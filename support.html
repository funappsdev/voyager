Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a trip?

Tap the + button on the home screen. Add a destination, dates, and start building your itinerary.

How do I track my budget?

Open the menu and tap Spend Tracker to see a breakdown of estimated spend by category.

How do I sync trips to my calendar?

Tap the bell icon in any trip and enable Calendar Sync. Events will appear in your iPhone calendar.

Can I change the currency?

Open the menu, go to Preferences, and tap the currency badge to choose from 20+ currencies.

Is my data backed up?

All data is stored locally on your device. Use the Export feature in the app to keep a backup.

How do I enable dark mode?

Open the menu and toggle Dark Mode at the top of the panel.
